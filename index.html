Frequently Asked Questions

How fast will my medicine be delivered?

MediRun delivers most medications within 60-90 minutes of placing your order. For rare medications, delivery may take up to 3 hours as we source from multiple pharmacy partners to fulfill your needs.

Can I order prescription medications through MediRun?

Yes! You can upload your prescription directly through the app. Our system will verify it and process your order. For security, you'll need to present the original prescription to the delivery rider.

What payment methods are accepted?

MediRun accepts cash on delivery, GCash, and all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, JCB). We'll be adding more payment options soon!

Is there a delivery fee?

Yes, a small delivery fee is charged based on your location. Standard fee is ₱50, but MediSprinter subscribers enjoy discounted or free delivery depending on their plan.
