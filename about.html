About MediRun

Revolutionizing Medicine Delivery in the Philippines

Our Story

MediRun was born from a simple yet powerful realization: in a world where almost everything can be delivered to your doorstep, why should medicine be any different?

Founded in 2025 by a team of healthcare professionals, tech enthusiasts, and logistics experts, MediRun aims to bridge the gap between traditional pharmacy services and the digital age.

Our Vision

To create a society where healthcare accessibility is never a barrier, and where medicine delivery is as common and reliable as any other essential service.

Our Mission

To provide fast, reliable, and accessible medicine delivery services across the Philippines, ensuring that no one has to compromise their health due to mobility constraints, time limitations, or geographic barriers.

Nationwide Coverage

MediRun operates across major regions in the Philippines—Luzon, Visayas, and Mindanao—with plans to expand to even more remote areas.

Pharmacy Partnerships

We've partnered with leading pharmacy chains like Mercury Drugstore, Watsons, Southstar, Rose Pharmacy, and Generika to ensure you have access to a wide range of medications.

Tech Innovation

Our platform is built on cutting-edge technology that ensures a seamless ordering process, real-time tracking, and secure transactions.
